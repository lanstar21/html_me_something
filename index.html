My final fantasy brave exvius page

Welcome to my Final Fantasy Brave Exvius page! Here I will tell everyone about all the cool units I have including many 7 star units. Without further ado, here is a list of my units:

7 star Hyoh

This is my most powerful unit sitting at 1700 atk. He's level 115 right now, and I am working on a fire weapon for him. All I need is a fire weapon, and a second martial glove, and his build will be complete.

7 star Awakened Rain

This is my most powerful tank unit. With more than 10k hp, he's an absolute beast! He can guard the whole team against magic attacks, has a unique sword, and he has quite a few spirit boosting materia.

6 star Tidus

My Tidus is fully decked out in tdh gear. He can reach 1400 atk. The only downside is his lack of chaining partners. Still, he is a force to be reckoned with, and one of my favorite units to play.
